## About

I build systems that shouldn't break — and when they do, I want to know *why* in under a millisecond. Most of my work sits at the seams: **low-latency network paths**, **partitioned storage**, **real-time pipelines**, and the **observability** that tells you what actually happened. I lean toward C++, Go, and Rust when it matters, and TypeScript or Python when shipping fast matters more.
